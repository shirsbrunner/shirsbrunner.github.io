## Typeface-Info
Still a little rough on the edges, this is even darker than Ultra. Corners are not polished and there are some glyphs missing (mainly special signs), but I think it could do nicely for headings on this page.

## Things to look out for
Easier to kern (cough, numbers, cough) and space than other typefaces, this mainly lackes in forms and glyphs. I built this using corner components and I need to iron out some edges...
