## Life, Work and Projects
I just had some weeks of holidays in Japan with the family. The contrasts between technology and nature/spiritual things still amazes me. The same is true for the contrast between the huge companies and shiny skyscrappers and the small udon shops in the back alleys. Making large and modern things but keeping the love for small and sometimes imperfect things. 

I currently work for [we are cube³](https://wearecube.ch), the UX-division of [Puzzle ITC](https://puzzle.ch).
In the last half year, I've been pushing a lot of energy into work and into related discussions. In hindsight, this took up a lot of space in my mind and also a lot of creative energy. I eventually got the growing feeling that I haven't made enough silly things in ages. Thus, in the evening, I currently feel like adding little things and details to this page. I hope that things will change as I'll soonish switch jobs and go work as inhouse designer for Swiss Post.

Besides work and this page, I'm working on a new typeface with lots of dark pixels and I hope I can use it here. Furthermore I have some ideas for stupid webapplications. Let's see, what I can do.
