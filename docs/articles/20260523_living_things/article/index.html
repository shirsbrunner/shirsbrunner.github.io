Making Kombucha

23 May, 2026

290 words

State: More of an Idea

tags: cooking

Fermentation or something

Healthy for you, they say, easy, they say, makes you grow to more than 2 meters tall, they say. Summer is the time to try alchemy again. I follow more or less this how to make kombucha recipe. Seems easy, you build a scoby, feed it tea, collect kombucha, put it in bottles, make sure they don't explode, profit.

Log of events

Setup

The hardest thing when growing your own Scoby seems buying the "right" kombucha.

  • Non pasteurized
  • Non flavoured I found a bottle of greentea-kombucha that might be suitable in a store selling organic goods.

Next quest: Finding big enough jars to build a home-kombucha factory. While this seemed tricky at first, I found 3-4 suitable ones in one supermarket. Living in Switzerland? I suggest you go to Coop. I went for one with a little spout to better fill bottles with the strategy to use it as a water dispenser at parties should everything go wrong.

2026-03-23 Building a scoby, first try

After boiling water, already steeping tee in sweet water, I destroyed the big jar during cleaning with hot water. Sadly, shops are already closed over the long weekend. Time to make ice-tea. Or just using a smaller 1l glass from the basement. I hope the scoby does not need "enough" tea, even though I believe that less added kombucha might reduce the probability of success. Let's see how this develops.
